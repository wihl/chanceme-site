Learn Your Chances of Getting into an Elite College

The application process is opaque. Hard working students send off their applications and wait for months for a reply. We want to reduce some of that stress by showing the factors that influence your acceptance.

We have analyzed the acceptance results for 5000 students making 15000 college applications to the top 25 U.S. Colleges. From this we have built a series of statistical models to predict the probabilities of being accepted at an elite school. Try it out!
